The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is an independent film festival taking place for the first time during this August in Brooklyn, New York. It was started by Jennifer Viala, founder of Brooklyn Vibes Events Co. The film festival is free of charge and for all ages but requires pre-registration to secure a spot. The festival's mission is to help discover, expose, and promote indie filmmakers and promote Brooklyn as a center for cinema and tourism.
